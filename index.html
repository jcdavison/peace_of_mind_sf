Peace of Mind
Welcome, I'm Tim Richards, a Martial Artist and Athletics Coach. Peace of Mind is the manifestation of my life's progression to happiness through the path of athletics and martial arts.
My background has roots with the martial arts leaders and communities of Wing Chun, Jeet Kune Do, Small Circle Jiu Trap Boxing, Phillipino Stick and Knife Arts and many more. I am a Certified Strength Coach and I possess a deep knowledgebase in Excercise Science. Transforming people into content and happy beings through Fitness and Martial Arts is my passion.
Peace of Mind offers private and small-group classes in Martial Arts and Athletic Fitness. Group classes range $25-35 and private sessions approx $75/hr. I offer trades and discounts for many reasons and am open to negotiating many arrangements with passionate students. Classes meet at my Dojo near Post and Leavenworth during week days/nights, weekends and via private scheduling.
